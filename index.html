New York City is the Third Hottest City in the nation

By Kiara Thomas

September 30, 2021

New York City was ranked third in the country for urban heat island intensity, according to the Climate Central report released in July. Urbanized areas see an increase of more than 7.6 degrees compared to their surrounding suburban areas. Low income neighborhoods, primarily made up of Black and Brown people, are most affected by heat islands and have higher chances of getting heat-related illnesses and deaths. The South Bronx, Central Brooklyn and South Queens are among the areas with the highest heat indexes, according to New York City’s Department of Health.
